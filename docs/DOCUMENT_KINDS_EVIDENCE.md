# Document kinds — what each registry actually evidences

For every jurisdiction and legal form: which document proves the Registerauszug, the Gesellschafterliste and the Gesellschaftsvertrag.

**A document is the proof.** Structured data parsed out of a registry is not a substitute for the document that evidences it, so this table only credits a fact as provable when a retrievable document carries it.

Germany is out of scope: we source German entities directly, not through this supply chain.

## Legend

- **✅ Provable** — Evidenced by a document you already receive with the case.
- **💶 Provable on request** — Evidenced, but only by a document that must be ordered separately (additional tier).
- **❌ Not provable** — The registry does not evidence this fact. No supplier can change this — it is a matter of local law.
- **❔ Not yet researched** — We have not established the position. This is NOT a statement that the document does not exist.

## AT

### AG — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

Shareholders are not registered. Only a sole shareholder (Alleinaktionär) is recorded; otherwise the Aktienbuch is internal.

> **§ 5 FBG i.V.m. § 35 AktG (AT)**
> 
> „alle Anteile an einer Aktiengesellschaft alleine oder neben der Gesellschaft einem Aktionär"
> 
> Only in this sole-shareholder case is the Alleinaktionär registered (name, DOB/FN).
> 
> Source: <https://www.jusline.at/gesetz/aktg/paragraf/35> (retrieved 2026-07-17)

**Caveats:**

- Sole-shareholder case is the only exception; partial holdings never appear.


### GmbH — Gesellschafterliste

**✅ Provable**

No separate shareholder list exists. The shareholders are statutory content of the register extract itself, which is a base document.

**Document:** Firmenbuchauszug — delivered as `Registry Extract`

> **§ 5 Z 6 Firmenbuchgesetz (FBG)**
> 
> „Name und Geburtsdatum der Gesellschafter, gegebenenfalls ihre Firmenbuchnummer, ihre Stammeinlagen und die darauf geleisteten Einzahlungen"
> 
> Source: <https://www.jusline.at/gesetz/fbg/paragraf/5> (retrieved 2026-07-17)

> **§ 26 GmbHG (AT)**
> 
> „diese Tatsachen unverzüglich zum Firmenbuch anzumelden"
> 
> Registers each change as an event; this is the structural difference from the German §40 GmbHG, which maintains a list.
> 
> Source: <https://www.jusline.at/gesetz/gmbhg/paragraf/26> (retrieved 2026-07-17)

**Caveats:**

- FlexKapGG 2023: holders of Unternehmenswert-Anteile are NOT named individually — only a note that they exist plus the aggregate sum.
- WKO/USP checklists mention a founding 'Gesellschafterliste' attachment. It is court practice, not statutory (§ 9 Abs 2 GmbHG names only the Gesellschaftsvertrag and appointment documents), is never updated, and is NOT a §40 equivalent. The Hauptbuch entry is authoritative.


## BE

### BV — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

Only the founders at incorporation are public, via the Staatsblad extract. Later transfers are never published. The shareholder register is private.

> **art. 2:8 WVV**
> 
> „naam, voornaam en woonplaats van de … oprichters"
> 
> The extract of the oprichtingsakte published in the Bijlagen bij het Belgisch Staatsblad must state the founders, and shareholders who have not fully paid up their contribution. Founders at incorporation are public; later transfers are not.
> 
> Source: <https://vzw-wet.be/wvv/d1/b2/b2-t4/b2-t4-h3/b2-t4-h3-a1/b2-t4-h3-a1-o1/wvv-2-8> (retrieved 2026-07-17)

> **art. 5:24 WVV (BV) / 7:28 WVV (NV) — aandeelhoudersregister**
> 
> Kept at the registered office or electronically (eStox). Inspection right belongs to shareholders only. Not filed with KBO/BCE or the Staatsblad. KBO Public Search publishes no shareholders.
> 
> Source: <https://www.belgium.be/nl/online_dienst/app_kbo_public_search> (retrieved 2026-07-17)

**Caveats:**

- Sole shareholder (art. 2:8 §4 WVV): the identity is FILED in the company dossier at the Enterprise Court registry, but only the FACT is published in the Staatsblad. The dossier is consultable at the registry, so the identity is obtainable but not published. A company incorporated as single-shareholder from the outset has no such duty.
- SOURCE CONFLICT: an aggregated summary claimed §4 requires the identity to be published. The primary text and law-firm sources (Monard, Cazimir/Practicali) say filed, not published. The latter is trusted.
- UNVERIFIED: annual accounts (NBB VOL model) carry a 'Structuur van het aandeelhouderschap' note, but it reports only holdings notified under transparency rules (art. 7:225 WVV) — typically empty for an unlisted BV. Exact scope not extracted from the NBB PDF.


## CH

### AG — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

Shareholders are never public. Only the organs are registered. More opaque than AT: there is no sole-shareholder registration duty.

> **Art. 686 OR (Aktienbuch)**
> 
> The Aktienbuch is a private internal document, never filed with the Handelsregisteramt and not publicly inspectable. Only Verwaltungsrat, signatories and Revisionsstelle are registered.
> 
> Source: <https://www.baerkarrer.ch/userdata/files/publications/2023/mtxuqefqoqpq0epggnhq.pdf> (retrieved 2026-07-17)

**Caveats:**

- No sole-shareholder exception, unlike AT.


### Genossenschaft — Gesellschafterliste

**💶 Provable on request**

The only genuine Swiss analogue to a filed §40-style list — but only for cooperatives whose statutes impose personal liability or Nachschusspflicht. Deposited and open to inspection; not entered in the register.

**Document:** Genossenschafterliste (Art. 877 OR)

> **Art. 877 OR**
> 
> Members' list must be filed with the Handelsregisteramt where the statutes impose persönliche Haftung or Nachschusspflicht. Not registered, but deposited and inspectable. The internal Genossenschafterverzeichnis (Art. 837 OR) is not public.
> 
> Source: <https://www.zh.ch/content/dam/zhweb/bilder-dokumente/themen/wirtschaft-arbeit/handelsregister/genossenschaft/gen_merkblatt_fuehrung_genossenschafterliste.pdf> (retrieved 2026-07-17)

**Caveats:**

- Applies only to the minority of cooperatives with liability/Nachschusspflicht statutes.
- Not confirmed whether KYC.com can source this document.


### GmbH — Gesellschafterliste

**✅ Provable**

No separate list. Members are statutory content of the register extract itself.

**Document:** Handelsregisterauszug — delivered as `Registry Extract`

> **Art. 791 Abs. 1 OR**
> 
> „Die Gesellschafter sind mit Name, Wohnsitz und Heimatort sowie mit der Anzahl und dem Nennwert ihrer Stammanteile ins Handelsregister einzutragen."
> 
> Source: <https://gesetzestexte.help.ch/or/artikel.cfm?key=1118&art=Die_Gesellschaft_mit_beschraenkter_Haftung> (retrieved 2026-07-17)

**Caveats:**

- Not cantonal: register content is set federally (OR + HRegV); cantonal offices only administer. Zefix is the central index.


## CY

### Private limited — Gesellschafterliste

**✅ Provable**

The Registrar issues a certified Certificate of Shareholders/members showing name and address of each member, share class/value and number of shares. The Annual Return (Form HE32), filed on the public register, also lists the members/shareholders. Both are official obtainable documents evidencing the REGISTERED (legal) shareholders.

**Document:** Certificate of Shareholders/members (certified) / Annual Return HE32

> **DRCIP companies.gov.cy — Obtaining Certified Copies/Certificates (overview)**
> 
> „Certificate of Shareholders/members"
> 
> Listed among certificates the Registrar issues; outlines shareholder name/address, share class (value) and number of shares.
> 
> Source: <https://www.companies.gov.cy/en/business-entities/2-company/5-lifecycle/1-running-a-company/5-guidance/obtaining-certified-copies-certificates/overview> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Companies Law Cap. 113 — annual return provisions (Form HE32); DRCIP**
> 
> Every company must file an annual return (Form HE32) on the public register giving details of shareholders, directors, secretary, registered office and share capital, with audited financial statements.
> 
> Source: <https://www.companies.gov.cy/en/business-entities/2-company/5-lifecycle/1-running-a-company/5-guidance/obtaining-certified-copies-certificates/overview> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- CRITICAL nominee/beneficial distinction: the Certificate of Shareholders and the HE32 show only the REGISTERED (legal) shareholders. Cyprus widely uses nominee shareholders holding under declarations of trust; the registered member may be a nominee, not the beneficial owner.
- Beneficial owners are held in the separate UBO Register, NOT publicly accessible — only competent authorities and AML-obliged entities; not obtainable as an open registry document.
- Annual-return STALENESS: HE32 is a periodic snapshot (filed ~annually after the AGM) and may not reflect intra-year share transfers.


### Public limited — Gesellschafterliste

**✅ Provable**

As for private companies, the Registrar issues a certified Certificate of Shareholders/members and the public Annual Return (HE32) lists members, both evidencing the registered (legal) shareholders of a public company under Cap. 113.

**Document:** Certificate of Shareholders/members (certified) / Annual Return HE32

> **DRCIP companies.gov.cy — Obtaining Certified Copies/Certificates (overview)**
> 
> „Certificate of Shareholders/members"
> 
> Registrar certificate list; annual return HE32 required of every Cap. 113 company.
> 
> Source: <https://www.companies.gov.cy/en/business-entities/2-company/5-lifecycle/1-running-a-company/5-guidance/obtaining-certified-copies-certificates/overview> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- CRITICAL nominee/beneficial distinction: certificate/HE32 show REGISTERED (legal) members only; nominee holdings under declaration of trust are common and the beneficial owner sits in the separate, non-public UBO Register.
- For a listed/public company, freely traded shares can make the register a materially stale snapshot; beneficial holdings via nominees/custodians are not shown.
- INFERENCE: public-company treatment assumed identical to private per the single DRCIP certificate list.

**✅ Provable**

**Document:** Memorandum and Articles of Association (certified copy)

> **DRCIP companies.gov.cy — Obtaining Certified Copies/Certificates (overview)**
> 
> „Memorandum and Articles of Association"
> 
> Listed among the certified copies the Registrar issues.
> 
> Source: <https://www.companies.gov.cy/en/business-entities/2-company/5-lifecycle/1-running-a-company/5-guidance/obtaining-certified-copies-certificates/overview> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- UNVERIFIED: certified M&A copy appears obtainable by hand/post rather than via the online e-filing self-service list; delivery channel not fully confirmed from primary text.
- A certified copy reflects the constitution as amended up to its date of issue.

### Private limited — Registerauszug

**✅ Provable**

Cyprus has no single consolidated register extract. The DRCIP instead issues discrete certified certificates that together evidence the current registered structure (registered office, directors/secretary, capital, good standing). Each is an official document obtainable via e-filing or by hand/post, reflecting the company structure as notified to the register up to its date of issue.

**Document:** Certified certificates issued by the Registrar (Registered Office; Directors and secretary; Good Standing; Capital)

> **DRCIP companies.gov.cy — Obtaining Certified Copies/Certificates (overview)**
> 
> „Certificate of Registered Office Address, Certificate of Directors and secretary, Certificate of Shareholders/members, Certificate of Capital, Certificate of Good Standing"
> 
> Full enumerated list of certificates the Registrar issues.
> 
> Source: <https://www.companies.gov.cy/en/business-entities/2-company/5-lifecycle/1-running-a-company/5-guidance/obtaining-certified-copies-certificates/overview> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **DRCIP companies.gov.cy — Obtaining Certified Copies/Certificates (overview)**
> 
> „the certified copy/certificate shall reflect the structure of the company on the basis of what has been notified to the companies register up to its date of issue"
> 
> Governs currency/staleness of every certificate.
> 
> Source: <https://www.companies.gov.cy/en/business-entities/2-company/5-lifecycle/1-running-a-company/5-guidance/obtaining-certified-copies-certificates/overview> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- INFERENCE: no single German-style 'extract' exists in CY; the base document is a bundle of separate certified certificates, so which certificate satisfies REGISTERAUSZUG depends on the KYC data point required.
- The certificate reflects only what has been NOTIFIED to the register up to its issue date (potential staleness).

## CZ

### a.s. — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

For an a.s., shareholders (akcionáři) are GENERALLY NOT public: § 48 of zákon 304/2013 does not enter the shareholders of an a.s. into the obchodní rejstřík, and no shareholder list is filed in the sbírka listin. IMPORTANT EXCEPTION: where the a.s. has a single shareholder, that jediný akcionář IS entered under §48 and appears on the výpis. Beyond that, ownership is tracked outside the register (seznam akcionářů kept by the company, or the central depository for book-entry shares).

> **zákon č. 304/2013 Sb., § 48 — sole-shareholder a.s. entry (the only shareholder fact the register carries)**
> 
> „má-li společnost jediného akcionáře, zapisuje se i jméno a sídlo nebo adresa místa pobytu, popřípadě také bydliště, liší-li se od adresy místa pobytu, tohoto akcionáře"
> 
> Narrow exception; for a multi-shareholder a.s. no shareholders are registered.
> 
> Source: <https://www.podnikatel.cz/zakony/zakon-o-verejnych-rejstricich-pravnickych-a-fyzickych-osob/f5078374/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- EXCEPTION: a sole-shareholder a.s. — its jediný akcionář is on the výpis (§48). Overall status set to not_provable_from_registry because the GENERAL a.s. case has no registry proof of shareholders.
- Shareholders of a normal a.s. are recorded in the company-kept seznam akcionářů (registered shares) or the central securities depository (book-entry shares) — neither is part of the public register.


### s.r.o. — Gesellschafterliste

**✅ Provable**

KEY FACT CONFIRMED FROM STATUTE: for an s.r.o. the společníci (members) and the výše podílu of each member are entered directly into the obchodní rejstřík under § 48 of zákon 304/2013 and therefore appear on the výpis itself. No separate 'Gesellschafterliste' document exists in the Czech register: the internal seznam společníků under § 139 ZOK is kept by the company, not filed in the public register. Thus the current-members proof is the base register extract.

**Document:** výpis z obchodního rejstříku

> **zákon č. 304/2013 Sb., § 48 (skutečnosti zapisované do obchodního rejstříku u s.r.o.)**
> 
> „u společnosti s ručením omezeným jméno a sídlo nebo adresa místa pobytu, popřípadě také bydliště, liší-li se od adresy místa pobytu, jejích společníků, výše podílu každého společníka"
> 
> Members and their share amounts are register-entered facts and appear on the výpis.
> 
> Source: <https://www.podnikatel.cz/zakony/zakon-o-verejnych-rejstricich-pravnickych-a-fyzickych-osob/f5078374/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **zákon č. 90/2012 Sb. (ZOK), § 139 — seznam společníků**
> 
> The seznam společníků is maintained internally BY THE COMPANY and is not a public-register document; the register-side proof of members is the výpis under §48 of 304/2013.
> 
> Source: <https://www.kurzy.cz/zakony/90-2012-zakon-o-obchodnich-korporacich/paragraf-139/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- The výpis reflects the state as registered; there can be a lag between an actual transfer and its registration — the výpis proves the REGISTERED members.
- INFERENCE: the same logic extends to partnerships (v.o.s., k.s.) whose společníci are likewise register-entered under §48; not separately verified verbatim.


### a.s. — Gesellschaftsvertrag

**💶 Provable on request**

The a.s. constitution — the stanovy (articles/statutes), which for an a.s. also serve as the zakladatelské právní jednání — is filed in the sbírka listin of the obchodní rejstřík under § 66 of zákon 304/2013, together with its current full wording after amendments. It is a separate downloadable document, not part of the výpis.

**Document:** stanovy

> **zákon č. 304/2013 Sb., § 66 písm. a) — sbírka listin**
> 
> „zakladatelské právní jednání právnické osoby, statut nadace"
> 
> For an a.s. the stanovy are the zakladatelské právní jednání deposited in the sbírka listin; §66 also requires the current full wording after each amendment.
> 
> Source: <https://www.podnikatel.cz/zakony/zakon-o-verejnych-rejstricich-pravnickych-a-fyzickych-osob/f5078374/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- A very recent amendment of the stanovy may not yet be deposited in the sbírka listin, so the latest filed version can lag the actual current articles.

It is a separate downloadable document, not part of the výpis.

**Document:** společenská smlouva (u jednočlenné s.r.o. zakladatelská listina)

> **zákon č. 304/2013 Sb., § 66 písm. a) — sbírka listin**
> 
> „zakladatelské právní jednání právnické osoby, statut nadace"
> 
> The s.r.o.'s společenská smlouva / zakladatelská listina is the zakladatelské právní jednání filed in the sbírka listin; §66 also requires the current full wording after amendments.
> 
> Source: <https://www.podnikatel.cz/zakony/zakon-o-verejnych-rejstricich-pravnickych-a-fyzickych-osob/f5078374/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- The sbírka listin holds what the company has filed; a very recent amendment may not yet be deposited, so the latest version on file may lag the actual current constitution.

## DK

### A/S — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

Same limitation, WORSE for A/S than for ApS. The public Ejerregister again publishes only owners >=5% of capital or votes (Selskabsloven §58 stk.1 -> §55 stk.1); sub-5% shareholders are invisible and it is register data, not a filed shareholder-list document. The ejerbog (§50) is company-kept and, for an A/S, is accessible only to public authorities — NOT even to individual shareholders, unlike an ApS. It is not filed with Erhvervsstyrelsen. Consequently no full current shareholder-list document is obtainable from the registry. Additionally, aktier can be bearer/centrally-registered, further weakening any registry-based ownership picture.

**Document:** Det Offentlige Ejerregister (legale ejere) / ejerbog

> **Selskabsloven §55 stk.1 (notification threshold)**
> 
> „mindst 5 pct. af selskabskapitalens stemmerettigheder eller ... 5 pct. af selskabskapitalen"
> 
> Source: <https://danskelove.dk/selskabsloven/55> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Selskabsloven §58 stk.1 og stk.3 (public Ejerregister)**
> 
> „foretages registrering af de af selskabets kapitalejere, som besidder betydelige kapitalandele, jf. § 55, stk. 1"
> 
> stk.3: 'Oplysninger modtaget i henhold til stk. 1 og 2 offentliggøres i Erhvervsstyrelsens it-system.'
> 
> Source: <https://danskelove.dk/selskabsloven/58> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Selskabsloven §51 (access to ejerbog)**
> 
> „Ejerbogen skal være tilgængelig for offentlige myndigheder."
> 
> For an A/S the ejerbog is NOT made accessible to individual shareholders (that applies to anpartshavere in an ApS); it is not filed with CVR.
> 
> Source: <https://danskelove.dk/selskabsloven/51> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Selskabsloven §52 stk.3 (bearer/centrally-registered shares)**
> 
> A/S with ihændehaverbeviser or shares in en værdipapircentral are exempted from parts of the owner-book content rules, further limiting registry ownership visibility.
> 
> Source: <https://danskelove.dk/selskabsloven/52> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- UNVERIFIED as a complete shareholder proof: public Ejerregister shows ONLY owners >=5% — treat like a PSC-register limitation; sub-5% holders invisible.
- The public Ejerregister is register/parsed data, not a filed shareholder-list document.
- For A/S the full ejerbog is even less accessible than for ApS (public authorities only) and is not filed with the registry.
- Bearer / centrally-registered shares can make the true shareholder set indeterminate from any register.


### ApS — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

Denmark has two ownership records, and NEITHER yields a full current shareholder-list document from the registry. (1) The public Ejerregister on CVR/Virk publishes only owners holding >=5% of capital OR voting rights (Selskabsloven §58 stk.1 -> §55 stk.1). Owners below 5% are invisible, and it is a register data view, not a filed shareholder-list document — a PSC-register-style threshold limitation. (2) The ejerbog (owner book), which lists ALL kapitalejere, is created and kept by the company (§50), not filed with Erhvervsstyrelsen; for an ApS it is accessible to public authorities and to every anpartshaver (§51) but NOT to a third party such as a KYC requester. Therefore no full current shareholder-list document is provable from the registry.

**Document:** Det Offentlige Ejerregister (legale ejere) / ejerbog

> **Selskabsloven §55 stk.1 (notification threshold for legale ejere)**
> 
> „mindst 5 pct. af selskabskapitalens stemmerettigheder eller ... 5 pct. af selskabskapitalen"
> 
> Owner must notify company only at/above 5% of votes or capital; sub-5% owners never enter the public register.
> 
> Source: <https://danskelove.dk/selskabsloven/55> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Selskabsloven §58 stk.1 and stk.3 (public Ejerregister content and publication)**
>

## ES

### SA — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

A CURRENT list of SA shareholders is NOT obtainable from the Registro Mercantil. Registered (nominative) shareholders are recorded only in the company's private Libro registro de acciones nominativas kept by the company; bearer shares (acciones al portador) are even less traceable. Neither is filed at the commercial register.

**Document:** libro registro de acciones nominativas

> **Ley de Sociedades de Capital, art. 116 — Libro-registro de acciones nominativas**
> 
> „Las acciones nominativas figurarán en un libro-registro que llevará la sociedad, en el que se inscribirán las sucesivas transferencias de las acciones, con expresión del nombre, apellidos, razón o denominación social... La sociedad solo reputará accionista a quien se halle inscrito en dicho libro. Cualquier accionista que lo solicite podrá examinar el libro registro de acciones nominativas."
> 
> The book is held by the company, not the Registro Mercantil, and inspection is limited to accionistas. Bearer shares are not individually registered anywhere public.
> 
> Source: <https://www.iberley.es/legislacion/articulo-116-ley-sociedades-capital> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- EXCEPTION — single-member SA (sociedad anónima unipersonal): the sole shareholder's identity MUST be recorded at the Registro Mercantil (art. 13 LSC), so for a unipersonal SA the sole owner IS provable from the register.
- PARTIAL ALTERNATIVE — 'Certificación de titularidad real' gives UBOs >25%, not the legal shareholder list.
- Listed SAs report significant holdings to the CNMV, but that is a separate securities-regulator channel, not the Registro Mercantil.


### SL — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

A CURRENT list of SL shareholders is NOT obtainable from the Registro Mercantil. Ownership of participaciones is recorded only in the company's private Libro registro de socios, kept and custodied by the administrators; share transfers are documented by notarial deed but are NOT filed at the commercial register. Founders named in the escritura de constitución are on the register historically, but subsequent transfers are not, so the register does not evidence the present cap table.

**Document:** libro registro de socios

> **Ley de Sociedades de Capital (RDL 1/2010), art. 104 — Libro registro de socios**
> 
> „La sociedad de responsabilidad limitada llevará un Libro registro de socios, en el que se harán constar la titularidad originaria y las sucesivas transmisiones, voluntarias o forzosas, de las participaciones sociales... La sociedad sólo reputará socio a quien se halle inscrito en dicho libro."
> 
> Source: <https://www.iberley.es/legislacion/articulo-104-ley-sociedades-capital> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Ley de Sociedades de Capital, art. 105 — access restricted to socios**
> 
> „Cualquier socio podrá examinar el Libro registro de socios, cuya llevanza y custodia corresponde al órgano de administración."
> 
> The book is private to the company; only socios (not third parties/KYC requesters) may inspect it. It is not held by the Registro Mercantil.
> 
> Source: <https://www.iberley.es/legislacion/articulo-105-ley-sociedades-capital> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Ley de Sociedades de Capital, art. 106 — transfers by notarial deed, not registered at RM**
> 
> „La transmisión de las participaciones sociales, así como la constitución del derecho real de prenda sobre las mismas, deberán constar en documento público."
> 
> Transfers require a notarial public document but are NOT inscribed in the Registro Mercantil; they update only the private Libro registro de socios. Hence the register cannot show current ownership.
> 
> Source: <https://www.conceptosjuridicos.com/articulos/ley-sociedades-capital-articulo-106/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- EXCEPTION — single-member SL (sociedad unipersonal): the identity of the sole shareholder (socio único) MUST be recorded in the Registro Mercantil (art. 13 LSC), so for a unipersonal SL the sole owner IS provable from the register. This does not extend to multi-member companies.
- PARTIAL ALTERNATIVE — the Registro Mercantil offers a 'Certificación de titularidad real' (beneficial-ownership certification). This lists UBOs above the 25% threshold, NOT the legal shareholder list, and is not a substitute for a Gesellschafterliste.
- The escritura de constitución names the FOUNDING partners (art. 22 LSC), but founders != current shareholders once any transfer has occurred.

>

## LI

### AG — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

Shareholders (Aktionäre) of a Liechtenstein AG are NOT entered in the Handelsregister and do not appear on the extract. Namenaktien are recorded only in a company-internal Aktienbuch; Inhaberaktien are deposited with a Verwahrer who keeps a private register. Neither is public. The register lists only organs — never the owners.

> **Merkblatt zur Aktiengesellschaft, Art. 328 Abs. 2 PGR**
> 
> „Die Gesellschaft hat über die Eigentümer der Namenaktien (Aktionäre) ein Verzeichnis (Aktienbuch) mit den gesetzlich vorgeschriebenen Angaben zu führen."
> 
> Aktienbuch is kept BY the company, not filed with or held by the Handelsregister.
> 
> Source: <https://www.llv.li/serviceportal2/amtsstellen/amt-fuer-justiz/handelsregister/merkblaetter/004_merkblatt_zur_aktiengesellschaft.pdf> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Merkblatt zur Aktiengesellschaft, Art. 326a/326c PGR (Inhaberaktien)**
> 
> „Inhaberaktien sind beim Verwahrer zu hinterlegen. Dieser hat ein Register zu führen, in welchem sämtliche gesetzlich vorgesehenen Angaben über den Inhaberaktionär zu registrieren sind."
> 
> Verwahrer register is private (accessible to authorities), not the public Handelsregister.
> 
> Source: <https://www.llv.li/serviceportal2/amtsstellen/amt-fuer-justiz/handelsregister/merkblaetter/004_merkblatt_zur_aktiengesellschaft.pdf> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- Liechtenstein is privacy-oriented: AG shareholders are structurally non-public. Ownership reachable only via the non-public beneficial-ownership register (VwbPG) for authorities/obliged entities — never from the commercial register.


### Anstalt — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

An Anstalt has no shareholders; ownership/control sits with the holder(s) of the founder's rights (Inhaber der Gründerrechte) and, economically, the beneficiaries (Destinatäre). Neither is disclosed on the Handelsregister extract — founder rights are commonly held via a trustee, and beneficiary provisions typically sit in non-public Beistatuten. There is no register document evidencing the owners.

> **Merkblatt zur Anstalt (organisation)**
> 
> „Bei einer Anstalt mit Gründerrechten ist in der Regel der oder die Inhaber der Gründerrechte oberstes Organ."
> 
> The Merkblatt lists the entities that ARE registered (Verwaltung, Revisionsstelle, Repräsentanz) but does not list the Inhaber der Gründerrechte as register content.
> 
> Source: <https://www.llv.li/serviceportal2/amtsstellen/amt-fuer-justiz/handelsregister/merkblaetter/005_merkblatt_zur_anstalt.pdf> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Merkblatt zur Anstalt, Art. 536 Abs. 2 PGR (Begünstigte)**
> 
> „Die Statuten oder Beistatuten haben zudem auch nähere Bestimmungen über allfällige Begünstigte (Destinatäre) zu enthalten. Bei Fehlen einer solchen Regelung gilt die gesetzliche Vermutung, dass der Inhaber der Gründerrechte selbst Begünstigter ist."
> 
> Beneficiary provisions are placed in Statuten/Beistatuten; Beistatuten are not part of the publicly accessible register content.
> 
> Source: <https://www.llv.li/serviceportal2/amtsstellen/amt-fuer-justiz/handelsregister/merkblaetter/005_merkblatt_zur_anstalt.pdf> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- INFERENCE: the Merkblatt does not explicitly say 'founder-rights holders are NOT registered', but they are absent from the enumerated register content and Liechtenstein practice holds them via trustees; owners are therefore not evidenced by any register document.
- Beneficial ownership reachable only via the non-public VwbPG register.


### GmbH — Gesellschafterliste

**✅ Provable**

KEY POINT: unlike an AG, the members (Gesellschafter) of a Liechtenstein GmbH ARE entered in the Handelsregister together with their Stammeinlagen and therefore appear on the register extract — parallel to Austria/Switzerland. No separate filed Gesellschafterliste is needed; the register extract itself is the proof of current members. The internal Anteilbuch (share ledger) is a company-kept book, not the public proof.

**Document:** Handelsregisterauszug

> **Merkblatt zur GmbH, Art. 394 Abs. 1 PGR**
> 
> „Die Gesellschafter werden samt den gesetzlich erforderlichen Angaben und ihren Stammeinlagen im Handelsregister eingetragen."
> 
> The decisive provision: current members are register content, hence on the Auszug.
> 
> Source: <https://www.llv.li/serviceportal2/amtsstellen/amt-fuer-justiz/handelsregister/merkblaetter/007_merkblatt_zur_gmbh.pdf> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Merkblatt zur GmbH, Art. 402 Abs. 1 PGR (Anteilbuch)**
> 
> „Über die Stammeinlagen aller Gesellschafter ist ein Anteilbuch zu führen, aus dem die gesetzlichen Angaben zu den Gesellschaftern, der Betrag der übernommenen Einlage sowie jeder Übergang einer Stammeinlage und jede Änderung ersichtlich ist."
> 
> The Anteilbuch is an internal book kept by the company — NOT a publicly filed Gesellschafterliste; the public proof of members is the register entry/extract.

### P.S.A. — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

>

> 
> Source: <https://lexlege.pl/ksh/art-300-31/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Ustawa o KRS, art. 38 (PSA — sole shareholder note only)**
> 
> „w przypadku gdy spółka ma tylko jednego akcjonariusza - jego oznaczenie zgodnie z art. 35, a także wzmiankę o tym, że jest on jedynym akcjonariuszem spółki"
> 
> KRS/odpis notes only a sole akcjonariusz; it does not otherwise list shareholders.
> 
> Source: <https://lexlege.pl/krs/art-38/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- Full shareholder ownership of a P.S.A. is NOT provable from any public registry document; the rejestr akcjonariuszy is private.

- INFERENCE: the P.S.A. register's confidentiality mirrors the S.A. regime; precise wording sits in the PSA-specific provisions (art. 300(33)–(36)), confirmed only in part verbatim.


### S.A. — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

Shareholders of an S.A. are NOT public. The odpis discloses only a sole shareholder (jedyny akcjonariusz), if any. The current shareholder record is the electronic rejestr akcjonariuszy (which since 1 March 2021 replaced the former księga akcyjna), kept by an authorised professional entity (dom maklerski / bank / KDPW / notary). **Document:** rejestr akcjonariuszy (niejawny); odpis z KRS ujawnia wyłącznie jedynego akcjonariusza

> 
> „Rejestr akcjonariuszy jest jawny dla spółki i każdego akcjonariusza."
> 
> Register is open ONLY to the company and shareholders — not to the public and not part of the KRS.
> 
> Source: <https://lexlege.pl/ksh/art-328-5/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Ustawa o KRS, art. 38 (sole shareholder note only)**
> 
> The KRS/odpis notes only a jedyny akcjonariusz (sole shareholder); it never lists the general body of shareholders of an S.A.
> 
> Source: <https://lexlege.pl/krs/art-38/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

### sp. z o.o. — Gesellschafterliste

**💶 Provable on request**

A full current shareholder list IS provable, but NOT from the odpis. The odpis (base document) discloses only shareholders holding, alone or jointly, at least 10% of share capital, plus a note if there is a single (sole) shareholder. The complete current list is the 'lista wspólników' which the management board must file with the registry court after every change (KSH art. 188 §3); it lists every shareholder with number and nominal value of shares and sits in the akta rejestrowe (registration files), which are public and largely viewable online (Przeglądarka akt rejestrowych / RAR) for filings after July 2021.

**Document:** lista wspólników (w aktach rejestrowych) — odpis z KRS pokazuje wyłącznie wspólników >=10% oraz jedynego wspólnika

> **Ustawa o KRS, art. 38 pkt 8 lit. c (dane o wspólnikach sp. z o.o.)**
> 
> „oznaczenie wspólników posiadających samodzielnie lub łącznie z innymi co najmniej 10 % kapitału zakładowego oraz ilość posiadanych przez tych wspólników udziałów"
> 
> Only >=10% holders are entered in the KRS/odpis; holders below 10% are invisible on the extract.
> 
> Source: <https://lexlege.pl/krs/art-38/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Ustawa o KRS, art. 38 (sole shareholder)**
> 
> „jeżeli spółka ma tylko jednego wspólnika - wzmiankę, że jest on jedynym wspólnikiem spółki"
> 
> A sole shareholder is noted on the odpis, so for a single-shareholder sp. z o.o. the base document does prove 100% ownership.
> 
> Source: <https://lexlege.pl/krs/art-38/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Kodeks spółek handlowych, art. 188 §3 (lista wspólników składana sądowi rejestrowemu)**
> 
> „Po każdym wpisaniu zmiany zarząd składa sądowi rejestrowemu podpisaną przez wszystkich członków zarządu nową listę wspólników z wymienieniem liczby i wartości nominalnej udziałów każdego z nich"
> 
> This filed 'lista wspólników' is the full current shareholder list; it lives in the akta rejestrowe, not on the odpis.
> 
> Source: <https://lexlege.pl/ksh/art-188/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Ustawa o KRS, art. 10 (jawność / udostępnianie akt rejestrowych)**
> 
> Registration files (including the lista wspólników and umowa spółki) are public; viewable online via Przeglądarka akt rejestrowych / RAR for documents filed after July 2021, otherwise at the registry court.
> 
> Source: <https://arslege.pl/udostepnianie-akt-rejestrowych/k21/a11565/> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- CRITICAL: the odpis z KRS is NOT a complete shareholder list. Only holders of >=10% of share capital (alone or jointly) and a sole shareholder appear; dispersed/small holders below 10% are entirely invisible on the extract.
- The full current list = 'lista wspólników' filed under KSH art. 188 §3, held in the akta rejestrowe (an additional document, not the base extract).
- Online availability of the lista wspólników via RAR generally limited to filings after July 2021; older filings require a request to the registry court.

## SE

### AB — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

Bolagsverket does NOT issue any document evidencing current shareholders of an AB. The aktiebok (share register) is created and kept BY THE COMPANY under ABL chapter 5 and is never filed with Bolagsverket. It is nonetheless public: for a non-avstämningsbolag (which private ABs are), ABL 5 kap. 10 § requires the company to keep the aktiebok available at the company for anyone who wants to see it. So a current-shareholders document exists and is obtainable AS A DOCUMENT — but only from the company/its board, not from the registry. For KYC purposes it is not provable from Bolagsverket.

**Document:** aktiebok

> **Aktiebolagslagen (2005:551) 5 kap. 10 § (bolag som inte är avstämningsbolag)**
> 
> „aktieboken skall hållas tillgänglig hos bolaget för alla som vill ta del av den"
> 
> Obligation rests on the company (styrelsen), not Bolagsverket; the register is kept 'hos bolaget'.
> 
> Source: <https://aktiebolagslagen.nu/05-aktiebok> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Bolagsverket / statute — no shareholder document filed with or issued by the registry**
> 
> Confirmed against ABL ch. 5: the aktiebok is maintained by the company; Bolagsverket registers company name, board, signatories and share capital but not the list of shareholders.
> 
> Source: <https://lagen.nu/2005:551> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- The document (aktiebok) IS obtainable, but from the company itself, not from Bolagsverket — the registry issues nothing evidencing shareholders.
- INFERENCE: private ABs are non-avstämningsbolag, so 5 kap. 10 § governs; not independently verified per company.


### AB_PUBL — Gesellschafterliste

**❌ Not provable**

Bolagsverket issues no shareholder document for a publikt AB either. Which ABL rule governs depends on whether the company is an avstämningsbolag (typically listed/Euroclear), NOT on private-vs-public status. If an avstämningsbolag, ABL 5 kap. 19 § requires a printout of the aktiebok (max three months old, shareholders alphabetical, holders of at most 500 shares omitted) kept available at the company and at the securities central (Euroclear). If not, ABL 5 kap. 10 § applies. In every case the document comes from the company or the securities central, never from Bolagsverket.

**Document:** aktiebok

> **Aktiebolagslagen (2005:551) 5 kap. 19 § (avstämningsbolag)**
> 
> „en utskrift eller annan framställning av aktieboken hålls tillgänglig hos bolaget för alla som vill ta del av den. I en sådan utskrift eller framställning ska aktieägarna och förvaltarna tas upp i alfabetisk ordning. Utskriften eller framställningen får inte vara äldre än tre månader."
> 
> 'En aktieägare ska inte tas upp ... om hans eller hennes aktieinnehav uppgår till högst 500 aktier.' Register kept by Euroclear, not Bolagsverket.
> 
> Source: <https://aktiebolagslagen.nu/05-aktiebok> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Aktiebolagslagen (2005:551) 5 kap. 10 § (publikt AB that is not an avstämningsbolag)**
> 
> „aktieboken skall hållas tillgänglig hos bolaget för alla som vill ta del av den"
> 
> Fallback rule where a publikt AB is not an avstämningsbolag.
> 
> Source: <https://aktiebolagslagen.nu/05-aktiebok> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- CONFLICT-OF-CATEGORY: the split is avstämningsbolag vs non-avstämningsbolag, which does NOT map 1:1 to public vs private; avstämningsbolag status is set in the bolagsordning.
- For avstämningsbolag the printout omits holders of <=500 shares (5 kap. 19 §), so even the company/Euroclear document is not a complete shareholder list for small holders.
- In all cases Bolagsverket issues nothing evidencing shareholders.


### HB — Gesellschafterliste

**✅ Provable**

Unlike an AB, a handelsbolag has no aktiebok. Its partners (bolagsmän) are themselves registered facts in Handelsregistret and appear on the registreringsbevis. Bolagsverket notifies each person when registered/deregistered as a bolagsman. Therefore the current owners/partners ARE evidenced by the registry's base document.

**Document:** bolagsmän (registreringsbevis / handelsregistret) — delivered as `Partners named in the register extract`

> **Bolagsverket, 'Bolagsmän i handelsbolag' and 'Ändra bolagsmän i handelsbolag'**
> 
> Bolagsmän are registered in Handelsregistret; changes to partners must be registered, so the register/registreringsbevis reflects the current partners.
> 
> Source: <https://bolagsverket.se/foretag/handelsbolag/drivahandelsbolag/andrabolagsmanihandelsbolag.879.html> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- The register evidences the identity of partners (bolagsmän); it does not express fractional ownership/profit shares, which live in the (unfiled) bolagsavtal.


### KB — Gesellschafterliste

**✅ Provable**

No aktiebok exists. The partners of a kommanditbolag — the general partner(s) (komplementär) and limited partner(s) (kommanditdelägare) — are registered in Handelsregistret and appear on the registreringsbevis, together with the registered limited-partner contribution (insats). The current partners are thus evidenced by the registry's base document.

**Document:** bolagsmän (komplementär/kommanditdelägare, registreringsbevis) — delivered as `Partners named in the register extract`

> **Bolagsverket, 'Bolagsmän i kommanditbolag'**
> 
> Both komplementär and kommanditdelägare are registered as bolagsmän; changes must be registered, so the register/registreringsbevis reflects current partners.
> 
> Source: <https://bolagsverket.se/en/foretag/kommanditbolag/startakommanditbolag/bolagsmanikommanditbolag.917.html> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

**Caveats:**

- Register evidences partner identity (and limited-partner contribution amount), not internal profit/ownership shares, which live in the unfiled bolagsavtal.


### AB — Gesellschaftsvertrag

**💶 Provable on request**

The bolagsordning must be filed with Bolagsverket at registration and every amendment must be registered to take effect. As a document that has 'kommit in' to the authority it is an allmän handling (public document) and anyone may order a copy from Bolagsverket. It is a separate registered document from the registreringsbevis, hence proved_by_additional_document.

**Document:** bolagsordning — delivered as `Articles of association`

> **Bolagsverket, 'Bolagsordning för aktiebolag' + 'Allmänna handlingar'**
> 
> „En handling är allmän om den förvaras hos en myndighet och om den har kommit in till myndigheten eller upprättats där. Du har rätt att begära ut allmänna handlingar från Bolagsverket enligt tryckfrihetsförordningen"
> 
> A copy of the bolagsordning must be submitted at registration and all changes must be registered; being filed, it is orderable from Bolagsverket.
> 
> Source: <https://bolagsverket.se/sokforetagsinformation/kopioravhandlingar.2003.html> (retrieved 2026-07-18)

> **Bolagsverket, 'Bolagsordning för aktiebolag'**
> 
> A copy of the articles of association must be submitted with the application for registration; all changes must also be registered with Bolagsverket.
> 
> Source: <https://bolagsverket.se/en/foretag/aktiebolag/startaaktiebolag/bolagsordningforaktiebolag.483.html> (retrieved 2026-07-18)
